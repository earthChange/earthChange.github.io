Pay to Play

The ECVM issues 1 earthChange for each M² of that Land.

You may buy any amount of earthChange with money. earthChange does not represent property ownership and conveys no rights of governance and is not redeemable for any good or service. The DAO must use the money from this coin sale to buy that parcel.

Each Product_Ticket represents a single instance of a specific good or service, and therefore requires unique Land_Ownership and Work_Contracts for that production. Each Product_Ticket is initially-invalid and finally-expiring, with a "Window of Validity" defined by real limitations of production and storage required for that good or service.
